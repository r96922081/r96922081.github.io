Design Algorithm by Induction

Reference: Introduction to Algorithms - A Creative Approach by Udi Manber

  • Algorithm design using the analogy to mathematical induction

    1. Solve a small instance of the problem (the base case)

    2. Construct solution from solutions of smaller problems (the inductive step)

  • Concentrate on extending solutions of small problems to solutions of large problems rather than solve it from scratch

  • Challenge is to find a way to reduce the problem

  • It's not a universal approach, but it led author to develop new algorithms more quickly
