Why I prefer Go to C++/Python (in most cases)?

Advantages compared with C++

  1. Readability
  2. Easy to learn, easy to use
  3. Productivity

Advantages compared with Python

  1. Readability (brace is better than indention)
  2. Fast
  3. Statically typed
  4. Productivity (Python is only productive in a toy program, I always suffered when programs grow, say about 200 lines)
  5. Strong tooling (better IDE support, auto-formating (go fmt), lint (go vet))
